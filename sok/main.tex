\section{Benchmark harness}
\label{app:bench}

The first-party measurements in this paper come from two harnesses
in this paper's source tree:

\begin{description}
  \item[\texttt{benchmarks/c1/} --- the C1 (PLONK) harness.] The C1
    deployment under measurement consists of \emph{five Soroban
    contracts}, one policy per contract, with each contract holding
    two relations (membership and update) compiled at up to three
    circuit-size tiers; all verifying keys derive from a single SRS
    --- the Ethereum Foundation KZG Summoning
    Ceremony~\cite{eth-kzg-ceremony} --- whose provenance is
    hash-chained through the build (\S\ref{sec:cfg-c1}). The numbers
    populating Table~\ref{tab:cost}'s C1 sub-rows are extracted from
    the published \texttt{onymchat/onym-contracts} testnet release
    body for tag \texttt{v0.0.5} (captured 2026-05-02), via the
    extraction script \texttt{benchmarks/c1/extract.sh}. The release
    body's JSONL companion (\texttt{gas-benchmarks-v0.0.5.jsonl})
    carries the full \texttt{fee\_charged} breakdown
    (resource / non-refundable / refundable) per op-row;
    \texttt{methodology.md} documents the measurement contract
    (what is and is not timed; hardware and software pinning; the
    per-contract \texttt{results.json} schema; the SRS-pinning hash
    chain). Per-call sampling instrumentation within a single proof
    is deferred pending a dedicated benchmark binary; the current
    harness reports a coarse \texttt{wall\_clock\_ms\_total} per
    contract.

  \item[\texttt{benchmarks/c7/} --- the C7 (FRI feasibility)
    harness.] The C7 row is measured against a Stellar-testnet
    deployment of the FRI-style verifier from the
    \texttt{wt-pq-fri-feasibility} branch of the
    \texttt{onym-contracts} repository. The numbers populating
    Table~\ref{tab:cost}'s C7 rows are extracted from the
    \texttt{scripts/bench-gas/results-pq.\{md,jsonl\}} pair produced
    by \texttt{scripts/bench-gas/run-pq.sh} on that branch (captured
    2026-05-02), via \texttt{benchmarks/c7/extract.sh}. Only deploy
    and admin operations measure end-to-end on the feasibility
    branch; \S\ref{sec:cfg-c7} discusses why proof-bearing rows do
    not.
\end{description}

The two harnesses' extraction scripts are independent: \texttt{c1}
parses the public GitHub release body; \texttt{c7} parses a JSONL
file produced by a re-run of the contracts-repo benchmark driver.
\texttt{benchmarks/README.md} documents both flows step-by-step,
including how to refresh the cells if the contracts repo cuts a new
release.
